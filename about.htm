About Us

Project Dahan was founded by two medical students, Swarnim and Sanam, who witnessed the silent suffering of many vulnerable individuals in their community.

We aim to fight against manipulation-based sexual exploitation, trafficking, bullying, hate, misinformation, and substance abuse by building a safe, compassionate, and action-driven platform.
